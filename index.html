My Interests

Here are a few hobbies/interests of mine. Theres a good chance some of these won't be relevant to my career, but since thought patterns in part determine brain development, I thought it might be worth mentioning.

Coding/Programming

I have always enjoyed coding, ever since I found the TI-BASIC programming interface on my dad's old TI-83.

Security

Security has always been important, but will become more importand still as probabilistic security paradigms become outmoded.

Robust-First Computing

Even when putting aside the security facet of this paradigm, it is becoming clear that many industries will have to completely rethink their design philosphies in the future. This becomes extremely important for computational tasks such as russia's "dead hand" initiative.

Open Source Resources

Ray Kurtzweil's Law of Accelerating Returns is inherently based on the assumption that humans will continue to develop new and more useful tools for all to use.

Energy

There are a variety of problems in need of solutions involving converting energy into a useable format, transporting it, and storing it. As we shift away from fossil fuels, meeting demand will become more challenging. Solar and wind power are not always available, so people like Elon Musk are beginning to implement partial solutions the first of these issues, which is energy storage.

Accessibility

Biological impairment often result in profound and sudden changes in an individuals capacity and quality of life. This, however, will change for millions all across the world in the coming years.

Body-Machine Integration

With new and exiting advances in areas like brain-computer interfacing, it seems that the relationship between man and machine will undergo a fundimental change in the relatively near future. Commercially available intraveinous brain electrodes will become available as soon as 2017.

Data Management

The total amount of information that is collected every day continues to increase at a growing rate. New advances in a variety of fields will be neccessary to maintain the database systems of the future.
